Together, we created a set of principles describing how we deliver our best work as product managers at DfE.

  1. We set the vision, missions and strategy for our products, and share it. We know how this fits into the bigger picture.
  2. We know and understand our users and customers. We act as their voice.
  3. We don't deliver outputs, we deliver outcomes. We're accountable for them. We make sure they're measurable, attainable and valuable.
  4. We align and prioritise our work towards the outcomes, using a roadmap.
  5. We identify our stakeholders and take them on a journey with us. We work with them, not at them.
  6. We do the hard work so that everyone on our team knows what's expected of them.
  7. We're evidence led. We don't let our own preferences guide our thinking. We know our products better than anyone.
  8. We help raise the bar for Product Management. We help people where we can and we are not afraid to ask for help ourselves. We are bold.
