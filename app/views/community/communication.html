Our Product Management team in DfE Teams is a great place to meet, speak to and get advice from other DfE product managers.

There might also be a chat or team for product managers in your area of DfE - ask your lead product manager.

Head of Profession email update

Debbie sends a regular email with updates on the community, recruitment and work that the Heads of Profession are doing. Contact Debbie in DfE Teams or email debbie.blanchard@education.gov.uk to get on the distribution list.

Cross government Slack

You can also join the cross-government Slack channel #prodmgt (opens in new tab) to meet and talk to product managers from other government departments. There are useful links pinned in the channel, including learning lists and a buddy scheme you can sign up to.

Anyone with a .gov.uk email address can sign up for cross government Slack (opens in new tab).
