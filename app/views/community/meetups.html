Our community meets online every Thursday from 1:30pm to 2:30pm. You can view the programme for future meetups (DfE users only).

To get the invite, contact Debbie Blanchard in DfE Slack or email debbie.blanchard@education.gov.uk.

We have different types of meetups including:

  • show and tells - where DfE product managers share what they are currently working on
  • guest speakers - who may be from outside of DfE or a member of a different profession
  • action learning - where we work on something together as a community
  • content conclave - where as a group we discuss our thoughts on a piece of content about product management
  • joint sessions - where we join up with other profession communities

Get involved with meetups

You can volunteer to lead a session by adding to programme for future meetups (internal only). To suggest a topic or speaker you'd like to see at a future meetup, start a discussion in our Slack channel or contact Debbie Blanchard.
