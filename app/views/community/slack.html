Our #productmanagement (opens in new tab) channel in Department for Education (DfE) Slack is a great place to meet, speak to and get advice from other DfE product managers.

There might be a product manager channel in your area. Ask your lead product manager.

All civil servant and contractor product managers should be given access to Slack when you join DfE. If not, you can sign up manually for Slack (opens in new tab). You will need an education.gov.uk email address to join.

Cross government Slack

You can also join the cross-government Slack channel #prodmgt (opens in new tab) to meet and talk to product managers from other government departments. There are useful links pinned in the channel, including learning lists and a buddy scheme you can sign up to.

Anyone with a .gov.uk email address can sign up for cross government Slack (opens in new tab).
