Debbie Blanchard

Head of Product Management

Debbie's role is to:

  • provide strategic leadership
  • be the expert practitioner and provide coaching and support to others
  • develop standards and tools
  • develop product managers professional capacity
  • lead the Department for Educatio (DfE) product community and represent us across government

Contact Debbie in DfE Slack or email debbie.blanchard@education.gov.uk.

Lead product managers

We have lead product managers in different areas of DfE.

Lead product managers:

  • lead the product tactics and strategy for a portfolio or end-to-end service
  • apply expert product management skills within teams at portfolio level and are responsible for defining the strategic direction and alignment to the strategy
  • are a member of the management team for their product area and line manage product managers in their area
