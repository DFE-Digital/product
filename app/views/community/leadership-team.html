Head of Profession

Debbie Blanchard is the Head of Profession for Product Management.

Her job is to:

  • provide strategic leadership
  • be the expert practitioner and provide coaching and support to others
  • develop standards and tools
  • develop product managers professional capacity
  • lead the Department for Education product community and represent us across government

You can contact Debbie in DfE Slack or email debbie.blanchard@education.gov.uk.
