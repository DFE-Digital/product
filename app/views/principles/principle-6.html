Working in a multi disciplinary team can be tough. Whilst a lot of work on team dynamics sits with the delivery manager, product managers should also get involved.

Building trust with your delivery manager and wider team will help. Make sure they know that you are the one responsible for the risks that the team take, and that you will pick up any fallout if it's the wrong decision.

As product managers, we're responsible for making sure that things are done in the right way. You should know enough about each discipline to be able to make an intelligent challenge.

Make sure the team are aware of the standards they have to meet including:

  • The Service Standard
  • accessibility standards
  • The Technology Code of Practice
  • DfE Standards

Drive best practice to create the best product you can. Address problems as they arise and model the positive culture that you want to see on your team.
