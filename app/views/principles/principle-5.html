What we want

  • An agreed group of stakeholders who buy-in to the product vision and roadmap
  • Stakeholders who understand how digital works, and who give us space to help them achieve their outcomes
  • Alignment across stakeholders in terms of what will get done, and when
  • Clear engagement at show and tells

How we do this

  • Engage early: get to know your stakeholders from day one. Actively build and maintain these relationships through shuttle diplomacy (regular 1:1 sessions with key stakeholders)
  • Tailor communications and conversation – find a common language to determine outcomes
  • Complete a stakeholder matrix and review it regularly
  • Include stakeholders in your conversations about vision, strategy, roadmap etc.
  • Be collaborative.
  • Be transparent about what’s being worked on and why, as well as any problems you’re encountering
  • Remain available and approachable to all colleagues and be receptive to new ideas.

What to avoid

  • Don’t avoid challenge or dis-alignment, tackle it
  • Don’t leave it late to engage
  • Gaps in talking to stakeholders – it is not a once and done thing
  • Don't assume you know what your stakeholders need
  • Don’t listen to stakeholder opinion over user and business needs
  • Glossing over problems or not talking about them
