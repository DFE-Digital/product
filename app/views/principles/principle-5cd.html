Your aim is to have an agreed group of stakeholders who:

  • are bought-into your product vision and roadmap
  • understand how digital delivery works, and give you space to help them achieve their outcomes
  • are aligned in their understanding of what will get done and when
  • engage with you at show and tells

How we do this

You should complete a stakeholder matrix and review it regularly.

Get to know your stakeholders from day one. You should actively build and maintain these relationships through shuttle diplomacy (regular 1:1 sessions with key stakeholders). Stakeholder engagement is not once and done, you should talk to them regularly.

Do not assume you know what your stakeholders need.

You should tailor your communications and conversation to your stakeholders. You'll need to find a common language to determine outcomes.

You should work collaboratively with your stakeholders. Include them in conversations about your vision, strategy and roadmap.

Do not listen to stakeholder opinion over user and business needs.

Remain available and approachable to all colleagues, and be receptive to new ideas.
